All membership options are subject to a one-off joining fee payable at the time of the induction.

The flexibility of a Pay As You Go gym with the personal service of a ‘Members only’ gym.

There is NO CONTRACT, therefore membership can be frozen, cancelled, changed at anytime by the member. Holidays and/or sickness of one month or longer can also be refunded.

When you join Gym & Slim we will create your own own tailor made fitness programme.

An induction will typically take one hour; its crucial that the instructor gains an indepth understanding of what goals the member has, what their current capabilities are, any physical limits they may have as well as their exercise history.

The member also has a full physical assessment, this will include Blood pressure monitoring, body fat, bmi, height, weight and body measurements (all with the exception of blood pressure are optional but encouraged), this really helps for ladies to see just how well they’ve done when they are re-assessed 10-12 weeks later.

The final stage is putting the member on the circuit and designing the programme specific to them (taking account of the information gathered) which of course is always carried out by a fully trained instructor. Once this is complete you’re a fully fledged member of Gym & Slim.

An instructor then continues to give 1:1 support for as long as they feel they need it. Even after this period there is always a gym instructor around to help, advise, support or just generally chat to so the 30 minutes go even quicker!
